### 2026-04-29

## Bug Fixes

- Sending a screenshot together with a question as the very first message in a session no longer makes the AI stop after a one-sentence "I'll look at the screenshot" stub. The same message used to be sent twice under the hood; now it's only sent once.
- The AI no longer occasionally echoes Claude Code internal markers like `[CONTEXT END] Continue the conversation naturally...` into a reply when a session is being resumed.
- On Mac (iPad app on Apple Silicon), the **X** button at the top-left of the image preview is now clickable with the mouse — you no longer need to press ESC to close it.
