## Improvements

- Listening mode now reliably keeps the microphone alive through long pauses and during the AI's reply. The waveform freezes in place while the AI is speaking instead of vanishing, and your speech captured before the reply started is preserved for the next round.
- Detecting that you've stopped talking is more robust — the app now combines transcript progress with raw audio level, so silence is correctly recognized even when you're speaking a language the recognizer wasn't tuned for, or when the recognizer briefly stalls.

## What's New

- Listening mode gets two new controls: a small **Stop AI** button to interrupt the agent without leaving listening mode, and a **Switch to keyboard** action that captures whatever you've already said and drops the transcribed text into the regular input field for editing.
