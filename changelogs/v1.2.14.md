## What's New

- Added "Sign in with Btelo.com" on the login screen. Btelo accounts that hold a Pro or Premium subscription are automatically recognized as vibe-remote Pro, alongside existing iOS App Store subscriptions.
- Login screen now has a language switcher and a theme switcher in the top-right corner, so you can change the language or light/dark appearance before signing in.

## Improvements

- Refreshed login button styling: Btelo button is black in light mode (white in dark), Apple and Google buttons are white in light mode (black in dark) for a cleaner, more consistent look.
