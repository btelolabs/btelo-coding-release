## What's New

- **Fixed: Push notifications now always arrive on the lock screen**, even when the app has been killed. Previously, notifications could go silent if the app was closed while a task was running.
- **Improved: Dynamic Island now updates to "Done" when a task finishes while the phone is locked** and the app is still in memory. The notification sound plays once — no double sounds.
- **New: Option to auto-archive old sessions**, keeping only the 9 most recent per directory. Enable it in Settings to keep your session list tidy automatically.
