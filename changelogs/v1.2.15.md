## Improvements

- Updated download, issue, and discussion links to the new `btelo-vibe-coding-release` repository.

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed an issue where upgrading to Pro on btelo.com after signing in on the app would not take effect until many hours later. Pro status is now picked up immediately when the app launches or returns to the foreground.

## Improvements

- Btelo subscriptions are now treated the same as admin-granted Pro days, so a temporary btelo.com outage no longer downgrades a paying user back to free.
