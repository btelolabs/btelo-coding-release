### 2026-04-28

## Improvements

- Listening Mode silence detection now ignores tiny trailing punctuation/casing tweaks the recognizer emits after you stop talking, so it actually fires reliably the first time you pause.
- The optional "Sent" voice cue no longer delays your message — it now plays after the send, in parallel with the next recording getting set up.

### 2026-04-27

## What's New

- **Listening Mode** — a hands-free voice coding mode for the chat input. Tap the new ∞ button to start talking; pause for a couple of seconds and the message sends automatically. The card shows a live waveform plus a countdown so you know when it's about to fire, and the AI's reply can be read aloud automatically before the next round starts.
- Listening Mode auto-detects Chinese, Japanese, and Korean for the spoken-reply voice, so multilingual replies sound right.
- Optional settings inside the listening card: auto-refine your transcript before sending, auto-speak the previous reply, and an audible "Sent" cue.
- Manual Stop while listening now captures whatever you said and drops the transcript into the input field for review, instead of discarding it.

## Improvements

- Listening Mode error messages (network, sign-in, subscription) now show right inside the listening card and clear themselves on the next round.
- The recording level threshold now adapts to your ambient noise floor, so soft sentence endings are no longer clipped during long recordings.

### 2026-04-26

## What's New

- **Mid-turn recovery for AI replies.** If the connection drops or you relaunch the app while the AI is still streaming a reply, you'll now come back to the full message instead of a stub — replies are persisted as they arrive and replayed when you reconnect.

### 2026-04-25

## Bug Fixes

- Sending a follow-up message while the AI was still streaming could occasionally clobber the new reply with leftover content from the interrupted turn, leaving the chat with duplicated or out-of-order messages. The interrupted turn now bows out cleanly.

## Improvements

- Stream debug logs now live alongside the agent's other logs instead of `/tmp`, so they survive reboots and are easier to find when investigating an issue.
