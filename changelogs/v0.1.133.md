## What's New

### iOS App

- **Connector switching** — switch between saved relay connectors directly from the app
- **Profile editing** — edit your nickname and avatar from the profile screen
- Enlarged login logo for a cleaner first impression
- Different icons for active vs. paused cron jobs
- Removed provider icon from profile; show year-month for signup date

### Subscriptions

- Overhauled subscription and entitlement models — removed the referral system in favor of a simpler, more reliable subscription flow
- Auto-transfer subscription ownership on account sync

### TUI

- Improved TUI pairing flow with clearer step-by-step prompts
- Better iOS session connection handling after pairing
