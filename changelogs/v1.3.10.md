## What's New

- The iOS app now officially supports seven languages: English, 简体中文, Español, Deutsch, 日本語, Português (Brasil), and Français. Português (Brasil) is a new addition.
- The marketing site at coding.btelo.com is now fully translated into the same seven languages, with search-engine-friendly URLs (for example, `/zh-Hans/pricing`, `/ja/doc/getting-started`). The home page, pricing, support, download, and documentation all honor your chosen language; blog posts, release notes, and the privacy/terms pages remain in English.
- A globe-shaped language switcher appears in the top navigation on every page — tap it to jump between languages without losing your place.
- If you previously selected a language that is no longer offered, the app now falls back to English on next launch instead of getting stuck on a missing translation.
- The documentation has been refreshed to match how the current app actually works: corrected outdated relay hostnames, added the missing `client` and `delete-connector` commands, documented the full set of TUI shortcuts (including the Logs and Devices screens), and added GitHub Copilot to the AI providers list.
